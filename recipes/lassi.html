Lassi

Description

Lassi is a delicious, homemade, and easy-to-prepare ice cream. It's made with a blend of cream, sugar, and fruit juices, such as strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries. The creamy texture is smooth and creamy, while the sweet and fruity flavor is balanced and refreshing. Lassi is often enjoyed with a scoop of vanilla.

Ingredients
  • ice cubes
  • 1 ¾ cups plain yogurt
  • 1 ½ cups ice water
  • 6 cubes ice, crushed
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
Steps
  1. Fill 6 tall glasses with ice cubes.
  2. Place yogurt, ice water, crushed ice, sugar, and salt in a blender; blend until frothy.
  3. Pour over ice cubes in the glasses to serve.
